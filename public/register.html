Terms & Conditions for Fest Registration

Welcome to CEREBREXIA'25 Fest Registration! Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before proceeding. By clicking 'Accept & Proceed', you agree to be bound by these terms.

What Your Fest Pass Includes:

  • Access to all Pro-Nite events (typically 2-3 nights, specific dates on schedule).
  • Entry to specified day events (cultural, academic, sports - check event details for any separate participation fees for competitive events).
  • Access to guest lectures and workshops (some workshops may have limited seats/additional fees).
  • Food court access (food & beverages are chargeable unless specified otherwise).
  • A CEREBREXIA'25 welcome kit (subject to availability, for early bird/full pass holders).

Registration & Payment:

  • The standard fest registration fee is ₹1600 per person (unless altered by valid coupons/offers).
  • Group enrollment: For every 8 members in a group who purchase the fest pass, one member gets free entry (i.e., you pay for 7 passes, the 8th is free). This applies to the base fest pass price.
  • Coupon Codes:
    • `IGIMSMBBS`: 40% off the fest pass for current IGIMS, Patna students (College ID verification may be required at entry).
    • Other coupons apply as per their specific terms. Coupons are single-use and non-transferable. Only one coupon per transaction.
  • All payments are to be made online through our secure payment gateway.
  • Registration is confirmed only upon successful payment and receipt of a confirmation email/QR code.

Event Access & Rules:

  • IGIMS, Patna students who have successfully registered for the Fest Pass (paid or via `IGIMSMBBS` coupon) will get free entry to participate in most day events. Some specialized workshops or premium events might have separate charges.
  • Students from other colleges will need to pay applicable participation fees for individual day events if they have not purchased the full Fest Pass, or if the event has a separate fee even for Fest Pass holders.
  • Attendees must adhere to all rules and regulations of IGIMS Patna and CEREBREXIA'25.
  • Entry is based on valid registration (QR code) and identity verification (College ID + Govt. ID).
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to deny entry or remove individuals for misconduct without refund.
  • Prohibited items, alcohol, and illegal substances are not allowed.

Refund & Cancellation Policy:

  • All Fest Registrations are strictly **non-refundable** and **non-transferable**.

By proceeding, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms and conditions.
