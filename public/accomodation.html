Accommodation Portal

Your Stay, Sorted for CEREBREXIA'25!

Welcome! We offer comfortable and secure accommodation options for participants traveling from out of town. Facilities are separate for male and female attendees. Limited spots available, so submit your request early!

Important Information

Accommodation Charges & Payment: Charges for accommodation are applicable per person, per night. The final amount and detailed payment instructions (including payment gateway link/method) will be communicated to you via email if your accommodation request is confirmed by our team. Payment must be completed within the stipulated time to secure your spot.

Accommodation Pass: Upon successful confirmation of your accommodation and completion of payment, you will receive an official CEREBREXIA'25 Accommodation Pass from our coordinators upon your arrival and check-in. This pass is mandatory and must be carried with you at all times while on campus and within the accommodation premises for identification and access.

Male Accommodation

Accommodation FAQs

What are the charges for accommodation? +

Accommodation charges are applicable per person, per night. The exact amount will be communicated to you via email after your request is reviewed and confirmed by our team. This email will also contain detailed payment instructions.

How do I make the payment for accommodation? +

Once your accommodation request is confirmed, you will receive an email with a payment link or other payment methods (like UPI/bank transfer details) and a deadline for payment. Please ensure timely payment to secure your spot.

What is an Accommodation Pass and why is it mandatory? +

The Accommodation Pass is your official identification for accessing the provided accommodation facilities. It will be issued by our coordinators upon your arrival and after successful payment confirmation. For security and management purposes, it is mandatory to carry this pass with you at all times within the accommodation premises and on campus when requested.

What should I bring for my stay? +

We provide clean bedding and linens. However, participants are advised to bring their own personal toiletries (soap, shampoo, towel, etc.) and a padlock for securing their belongings in the provided storage units.

Are there separate facilities for male and female participants? +

Yes, absolutely. We have separate and secure accommodation facilities for male and female participants, each supervised by respective wardens and security personnel.

What are the check-in and check-out timings? +

Standard check-in usually starts a day before the fest (e.g., Dec 14th if the fest is Dec 15-17) and check-out is typically the day after the fest concludes (e.g., Dec 18th). Specific timings will be communicated upon confirmation of your accommodation. Please adhere to these timings for smooth operations.

Can I choose my roommates? +

Accommodation is generally provided on a shared, dormitory-style basis. While we cannot guarantee specific roommate requests due to logistical constraints, you can mention any preferences in the "Special Requests" section of the form, and we will try our best if feasible, especially for groups from the same college.
